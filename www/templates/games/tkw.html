Rules

  • Rock, paper, scissors to start for side or ball
  • Start the game: Ball must start on the ground
    • 6 seconds to play the ball
  • Distribution:
    • NO drop kicks or punts
    • GK may not pass hand to hand, only hand to feet or feet to feet
  • After a goal: New ball from goal, must start on the ground
  • SUBS:Both teams MUST rotate after every goal scored
    • Can sub at any time [except if teammate is chasing]
    • Goalkeepers must stay in a consistent rotation throughout the game with GKs
  • GK can't cross half
    • First violation = warning
    • Second violation = GK must be subbed out
  • Last GK to touch the ball must chase it if it goes out of bounds
    • Team must play down a player
  • No player can touch the ball once it goes out of bounds except for the GK that last touched it.
    • Goal is award to other team if team assists or touch the ball in anyway

   Scoring
  • 1 pt a goal
   Dimensions
  • Adv: 24 yds x 20 yds
  • Youth: 22 yds x 20 yds
  • Total # of fields: 22 (16-adv + 6-youth)
