Rules

  • 2 GKs on the field at a time
  • Start the game: ball can be shot using punt, dropkick, thrown, kicked off of the ground or any other form of distribution.
    • 6 seconds to deliver
    • Only use feet when ball is not in the keeper arc.
  • After a goal is scored: ball can be shot using punt, dropkick, thrown, kicked off of the ground or any other form of distribution.
  • Ball sent off
    • Side line returns the ball to shooter (keep possession)
    • End line keepers ball (change possession)
  • SUBSboth teams sub when 2 total points are scored
  • REBOUNDS ARE LIVE
    • Points can only be scored on the team that gave up the rebound.
    • If a ball is collected by the team that gave up the rebound, they must go back to their arc.
    • Rebounds include shots off the frame that remain in the field of play.
  • ONLY 1 player from possessing team can leave arc to clean up ball
  • Upon retrieving a missed/spilled ball, retriever or teammate must use feet to shoot at keeper zone/goal.
    • Not from the hand

   Scoring
  • 2 pts a goal
  • 1 pt hitting the ground in arc [ball HAS TO BE held or SENT OUT of the arc on a fly]
  • 3 pts a goal that bounces in the zone first
  • REBOUNDS
    • goal = 2 pts
    • chipped into zone = 1 pt
   Dimensions
  • Adv: 32 yds x 18 yds w/ 8-yard arc zone
  • Youth: 28 yds x 18 yds w/ a 6-yard arc zone
  • Total # of fields: 11 (8-adv + 3-youth)
